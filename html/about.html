Camille is a pluridisciplinary artist based in Paris, France.
She uses photography, electronic music and coding as her mediums.

Camille was born and raised in Paris suburb. At 18 she moved to Guatemala where she stayed for eight years. She played in movies and then became a nightlife photographer. She has always been fascinated by electronic music and she started mixing after she played the part of dj Shasta in W2MW movie. As a multidiscilplinary person she pursue everything that she loves. In 2020 with the pandemic Camille started to nurture an true interest in web technologies and went back to school to learn webdesign. She is currently enrolled in a tech school to become a web developper.
